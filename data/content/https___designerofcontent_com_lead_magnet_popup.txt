Free Lead Magnet Popup Information

The Designer of Content website features a popup that offers visitors a free lead magnet. This popup appears after 3 seconds when a visitor first comes to the site (unless they've seen it before).

Key Features:
- Modern dark design with blue accents
- Appears after 3 seconds for new visitors
- Remembers if users have seen it (using localStorage)
- Collects name, email, and industry information
- Sends submissions to designerofcontent@gmail.com

Industries Supported:
- Travel Agents
- Realtors
- Salons
- Life Coaches
- Fitness Trainers
- Marketing Agency
- Home Cleaners
- Auto Repair Shops
- MLM Marketers
- Pet Groomers

The popup emphasizes three key benefits:
1. Choose your industry
2. Grab your free lead magnet
3. Start turning visitors into clients today

The form includes fields for:
- Industry selection (dropdown)
- Name
- Email

When submitted, the system sends an email notification and confirms to the user that their lead magnet is on the way to their inbox.

The popup is implemented as a WordPress plugin called "Designer of Content Lead Magnet" that adds the necessary HTML, CSS, and JavaScript to the website.
